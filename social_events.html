Social Events

For more information of any of the following you can contact our social sec Jane by phone on either 0151 6084575 or 07963 906979, or email via jane.croft1@ntlworld.com

Walk to Hilbre Island - Sat 13 August, 1.00pm by the ice cream shop next to the beach in West Kirby to start walking at 1.30pm

We aim to return by 4.30pm and then if anyone is interested in a meal at Surfers they do a teatime special of 2 courses £11.95 or 3 courses £13.50. Please let me know ASAP if they want to go for the meal. If weather is bad we plan to meet at 1.00 & just go for lunchtime special (£9.95 for 2 courses and a drink).

Club AGM, Oval Sports Centre - Sunday 15th July, at 5.00pm

Would you like your say on how your ski club is run; have you any suggestions or questions relating to the skiing sessions, training, teaching, holidays, racing or social events? Well this is your big chance. The Oval Ski Club's AGM is taking place on the 15th July at the Oval Sports Centre Meeting room (within the sports centre) at 5pm.
If that was not incentive enough the club will be providing free refreshments!!! (drinks and nibbles)

Remember the club is run entirely by volunteers without whom none of the above activities would take place, further volunteers are very much in demand, so if you can spare a few hours making drinks on a Saturday morning or would like to be Chairman (or any of the jobs in between) - any new volunteers would be extremely welcome.

All committee posts will be up for election.

If you interested in attending or would like more information please contact Jane by either phone or email (details at the top of the page).
