Welcome to Oval Ski Club!

Located in Bebington on the Wirral, We offer skiing tuition for all ages, an active social club promoting various events through out the year, and also a Ski Racing Team which all members are welcome to join. We are ideally located for the Wirral, Cheshire, Liverpool and Merseyside areas.

Closed for Summer

The 2011/2012 season has now finished. We will be busy over the summer months maintaining the slope so we are ready to open in September for next season. Check the sessions page for when we will be open and when we start new beginner lessons. See you then!

Never skied before? Don't let this stop you joining the club!

We provide lessons through out the season to all of our club members including people who have never skied before. For complete beginners these blocks of lessons start approx. every three weeks - see list below for starting dates. So if you are new to skiing and interested in joining the club then just turn up early on the starting date to enrole! See our club sessions page for more information.
